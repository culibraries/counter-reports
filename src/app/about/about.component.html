The COUNTER Reports Tool is a tool developed by the Core Technology & Applications, Collections Strategies and Metadata, Operations, Digital Asset Management and Discovery Services teams to perform the following tasks:

  • Automate the downloading of COUNTER reports
  • Provide a user friendly interface that allows library staff to download reports to make data-driven decisions.
